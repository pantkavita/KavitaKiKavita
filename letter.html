A Letter

DATE:-15 Aug 2020

Dear India ,
Firstly wish you a very happy birthday.As you know ,this year we are celebrating your 74 th birthday.With every passing year ,you are becoming more younger or more developed.Yes,I know that you have seen a lots of ups and down in your journey but this is the time to rejuvenate yourself,make yourself vibrant for the upcoming journey. We the people of India ,are trying to complete the pursuit of our predecessors and step by step we are also completing the dreams of Gandhi ji,Nehru ji ,Atal ji etc. Now the responsibilities of their dreams are on our shoulders and as a youth I will make sure from my side that their dreams will be our first priority for the whole life. And eventually,I just want to wish you a very good day and a great year.
With regards ,
Kavita Pant
